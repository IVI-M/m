Raison d’être

From the author

Dear friends, in front of you is not a regular book. It is the collections of dreams and feelings, transcribed into songs and music.

Music does not need translations, but it often comes with lyrics, in which case - translations are provided.

Welcome to the other side of the universe - not visible to the eye. Bonne voyage!

Yours
Dmitry Gorodnichy
